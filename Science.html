How Buzzbar fuels steady focus

Buzzbar is built around a simple idea: keep the **good** parts of caffeine (alertness, motivation) and smooth out the spike, jitters and crash.

The curve, not just the caffeine

Coffee and energy drinks hit hard, then dip. Buzzbar uses a lower, smoother caffeine dose plus L-theanine to stretch the focus window and make the experience feel more controlled.

  • Fast ramp so you actually feel it.
  • Flattened peak to reduce anxiety and jitteriness.
  • Slower decline, so you don’t crash mid-afternoon.

The stack behind the bar

We didn’t invent new molecules. We combined ingredients with decades of research behind focus, mood and performance – in a format you can throw in a bag and eat anywhere.

Lift

Green-tea caffeine

Natural caffeine from green tea for a cleaner, less edgy lift than high-dose coffee or shots.

  • Moderate dose aimed around ~80 mg per bar.*
  • Works in tandem with L-theanine for calmer alertness.
Smooth

L-theanine

An amino acid found in tea that helps take the “sharp” off caffeine and supports focus without feeling wired.

  • Helps reduce perceived jitters from caffeine.
  • Supports calm, sustained attention.
Hydrate

Electrolytes

Mild dehydration tanks focus. A small electrolyte blend helps your brain keep up with long days.

  • Sodium, potassium and magnesium in sensible amounts.
  • Designed for everyday use, not extreme sports drinks.
Resilience

Adaptogen support

Carefully selected adaptogens (like rhodiola / ashwagandha, final recipe dependent on testing) to help you handle stressy days.

  • Targets perceived fatigue more than “hype”.
  • Supports balance on top of caffeine, not instead of it.

*All values are pre-launch targets and may change as we finalize the recipe.

How it should feel in real life

Science is cool, but in the end Buzzbar has to do one thing: help you stay dialed in on the stuff that matters, without feeling cracked out or destroyed afterwards.

0–30 min

Wake up without whiplash

Smooth lift; you notice you’re more “on”, not suddenly buzzing.

  • Good for morning classes or a pre-work boost.
  • No shaking hands while you type.
30–120 min

Deep work window

Energy stays steady; pairing caffeine with actual nutrition means your brain has something to run on.

  • Great for exams, projects and long focus blocks.
  • Less tempted to snack every 20 minutes.
Afterwards

Less “post-caffeine regret”

The goal is to close your laptop and still feel like a human, not a zombie with a sugar crash.

  • Milder comedown vs. large coffees or energy drinks.
  • Protein & fiber help fill the gap between meals.

Quick questions

If we were sitting with you in the kitchen, this is what we’d expect you to ask.

Is this meant to replace coffee?

Not necessarily. For some people Buzzbar is the main caffeine source; others use it to avoid a third or fourth coffee.

Will you publish more data?

As we finalize the recipe, we plan to share more about exact doses and internal testing so you can see how we made those decisions.

What about tolerance?

If you’re very caffeine-sensitive, we recommend starting with half a bar. If you drink a lot of coffee, you’ll still feel it – but in a calmer way.
