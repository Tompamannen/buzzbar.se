One bar, different modes

You don’t need three different products. You need something that works across your actual day: study, work, training and everything in between.

Study Sessions

Stay switched on, not wired

Grab an Vero Bar on the way to lectures or before a library session. You get enough caffeine to feel a difference, plus protein and fiber so you don’t fade 45 minutes in.

  • Eat 20–30 minutes before you need full focus.
  • Pairs well with one normal coffee if you want a bigger kick.
  • Designed so you still feel human after a 3-hour block.

Why a bar, not a drink?

We love coffee. We just don’t love depending on it for every single energy gap. Vero Bar is a way to keep the ritual, but stop the chaos.

Coffee / Energy Drinks

Fast, strong, chaotic

  • Can spike hard, then crash just as hard.
  • No protein or fiber, so you still feel hungry.
  • Easy to overdo – especially late in the day.
Typical Snack Bar

Calories without clarity

  • Often lots of sugar, little actual nutrition.
  • No intentional focus stack, just carbs.
  • Can leave you sleepy instead of sharp.
Vero Bar

Built for focus first

  • Moderate caffeine + L-theanine “snack for thought”.
  • Protein, fiber and electrolytes for real fuel.
  • Meant for students, founders and anyone going all-in.

Where we are in the process
